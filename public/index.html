How to Use

1 Find Your Best Server

Click "Find Closest Server" to automatically detect your location and get the top 5 fastest servers ranked by actual network latency. You'll need to allow location access when prompted.

2 Browse All Options

Click "Browse All Servers" to explore all 500+ Mullvad WireGuard servers across 90+ cities worldwide. Expand any city to see individual servers and test their latency.

Pro Tip:

Lower latency (ms) = faster connection. Look for servers with <50ms for the best performance. Servers marked with OWNED are operated directly by Mullvad for enhanced privacy.
